\begin{abstract}
Understanding causal relationships in organizational time series data is critical for effective managerial decision-making, yet traditional forecasting systems often sacrifice interpretability for predictive accuracy. This research introduces \textbf{Causal Discovery Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks (CD-KAN)}, a novel information systems artifact that simultaneously achieves superior forecasting performance and discovers interpretable causal structures in complex business environments.
\end{abstract}
